How Detection Works — 4 Layers

1

Date Intelligence

Extracts any date from the claim. Past date + verified → REAL. Past date + impossible → FAKE. Future date → UNCERTAIN.

2

NewsAPI Live Search

Searches thousands of real-time news publishers. Matching article headlines are injected directly into Groq LLaMA 3.3 as evidence.

3

Google Custom Search

Cross-checks the claim against the broader web. Titles and snippets from credible sources are fed to Groq LLaMA 3.3.

4

Groq LLaMA 3.3 Final Verdict

Synthesizes all evidence (date + news + Google + its own knowledge) into a final weighted REAL / FAKE / UNCERTAIN verdict with confidence score.
